Bands

Bonny Abraham Ensemble

They are a 4-piece band from Cochin with hand pan, Oudh, percussion and vocals. This newly formed band is doing their debut performances at BIG 19.

Daniel Waples and Flavio

Daniel Waples is one of the world’s best-known handpan (hang drum) players having played in over 50 countries and amassing over 40 million views on YouTube. Daniel supports himself as an independent musician playing the handpan, an instrument associated with energy work and alternative healing. Flávio Emanuel Lopes, who is a violinist from Catalonia, join him in a duo act at BIG 19.

Derelicts

What would happen if you put 4 friends, with varied musical backgrounds and tastes, in a room with all their instruments? The Derelicts is the result of such a crazy experiment. Their sound can be described as funky and alternative with a tinge of 80s synth/dream pop. All they strive to achieve is have fun and share our joy with the masses by making em groove. The Derelicts play a unique blend of 80s inspired disco with modern pop, alternative & rock. Get ready to groove to the boys because they are funky fresh.

The F16s

The F16s are an alternative band hailing from the port city of Chennai, India. Their music, echoes the melancholia that grapples with the mundanity of everyday life in a town that serves very little outside of itself. Lyrically driven, the 'sound' of the band rests in an arc that also includes acts like LCD Soundsystem, Julian Casablancas and the Voidz and The Horrors; beat-driven yet innately melodic.

Mushroom Lake

Mushroom Lake is a psychedelic/post-rock/shoe-gaze band from India, formed in early 2011. The band was founded by Raju K P (guitars), Jitin David (guitars), Vijay Gopal (Keys/Bass), and Midhun Nishol(Drums). The band released their debut live album in the year 2014 which was received well by both local and international listeners. Mushroom Lake compose instrumental music with a heavy leaning towards post- rock mixed in a potion made of influences from psychedelic, alternative and other experimental genres.

Space Is All We have

Fast forward to 2019, and the band has taken their music to several music festivals and headlined college festivals alike. Having taken their time to perfect the sound in their heads, Space is now all set to pioneer a new genre of music with their debut album 'Thank You, Universe.' Their consistent live performances are a treat to both the eyes and ears, and they leave nothing more to be desired.

Yogev Haruvi

Played by one man altogether, aimed to get everybody move and dance with joy and happiness. Yogev Haruvi takes the crowd from wherever they are, on a journey to another space and time where everybody freaks out from the vibration of the didgeridoo and the spiritual power of the drums in one big unity and harmony. He is a a multi-instrumentalist from Israel.
